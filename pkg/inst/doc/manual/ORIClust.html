ORIClust
Second clustering analysis is based on the package ORIClust and its function ORICC1 and ORICC2 for one-stage or two-stage algorithm. Methodology is based within information criterion, clustering genes based on their profiles, while taking into account ordering of doses and monotonicity of dose-response relationship.
