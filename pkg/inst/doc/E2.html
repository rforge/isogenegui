Likelihood Ratio Test Statistic (E2)
This options provides the analysis using the E2 test statistics based on p-values obtained from its null hypothesis distribution. The provided adjustment methods are BH, BY, Holm, SidakSS, Sidak SD, Holm, Hochberg and Bonferroni. Based on the specified adjustment methods and overall significant level, the list of significant genes along isotonic means difference, test statistic value, raw and adjusted $-values, will be produced.
